About Me

I'm Faheem Iqbal, a passionate photographer from Shopian, Kashmir. My love for nature and storytelling through visuals inspired me to capture the beauty of our world — one frame at a time. I specialize in nature and street photography, and my work reflects the essence of everyday life and untouched landscapes. Let’s connect and explore creativity together!

Achievements

Over the years, my passion for photography has been recognized on both local and global platforms. I'm proud to have received 35 photography awards across various categories including nature, street, and conceptual photography and got into the list of Top 14% photographers all across the globe. These awards reflect my dedication to capturing authentic moments and stories through the lens.

One of the proudest moments in my journey was when my photo, captured on a Google Pixel phone, was recognized by Google and appreciated by their official Instagram handles. Their acknowledgment reaffirmed my belief that powerful storytelling can emerge from simple, everyday moments.

My work has also been appreciated by multiple online photography communities and has been published in local photo journals. I continue to explore new techniques and themes while staying rooted in the raw beauty of Kashmir and the everyday lives of people around me.
